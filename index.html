Welcome to Mussoorie

Explore the Queen of Hills — a paradise nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, offering breathtaking views, misty mornings, and serene landscapes.

About Mussoorie

Mussoorie, located in Uttarakhand, is one of India's most scenic hill stations. With its colonial charm, lush green hills, and panoramic Himalayan views, it's a favorite getaway for travelers. Whether you enjoy nature walks, cable car rides, or shopping at Mall Road, Mussoorie promises a delightful experience for everyone.

Top Attractions

Kempty Falls

One of Mussoorie’s most famous waterfalls, ideal for picnics and photography amidst stunning natural beauty.

Gun Hill Point

The second-highest point in Mussoorie, offering breathtaking cable car rides and panoramic views of the Doon Valley.

Lal Tibba

The highest peak in Mussoorie, known for its scenic sunrise and sunset views over the Himalayan ranges.

Camel’s Back Road

A peaceful stretch for evening walks, named after its camel-like rock formation offering mesmerizing sunset views.

Travel Information

Best Time to Visit

March to June is ideal for pleasant weather, while December to February offers snowy landscapes for winter lovers.

How to Reach

By Air: The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (60 km).

By Train: Dehradun Railway Station is the nearest railhead, 35 km away.

By Road: Well-connected by buses and taxis from Delhi, Dehradun, and Haridwar.
